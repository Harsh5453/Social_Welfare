Social Welfare Charity

The aim of social welfare is to focus on all the issues concerning human rights, social, environmental and advocacy. It work to promote and improve the social and political conditions of the society on a broad scale. Some of the functios of Social welfare is to provide education and feed underprivileged people and fulfill their daily need . It is a non-profitable charity .Millions of people give to charity on a regular basis to support causes they believe in, as well as for the positive effect it has on their own lives.
