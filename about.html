About Us


Here we represent our community which helps the needy persons . Every person feels the desire to be in an inclusive space surrounded by supportive people and when it comes to special needs, education and medical help go hand-in-hand. It is a non-profitable organization that works for the welfare of physicaly disability persons . We provide them education as well as help to students who are struggling with different kinds of needs. We also provide better treatment to needy person . and we arrange a job for our community member and we provide a low cost training courses to all intereted students. Thus help them attain economic independence.
